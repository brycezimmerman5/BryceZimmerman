    Zinedine Zidane

    Zinedine Zidane was born in June of 1972 in France. He is a retired French footballer and current manager of Real Madrid. He played as an attacking midfielder for the France national team, Cannes, Bordeaux, Juventus and Real Madrid.

    Ronaldo Luis Nazario

    Ronaldo Luis Nazario was born in Brazil on September 18, 1976. Currently he is a Brazilian retired professional footballer who played as a striker. he is widely considered to be one of the greatest football players of all time. In his prime, he was known for his dribbling at speed, feints, and clinical finishing.
